Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

In this project, researchers are searching for bacteria that can synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Since human body is the source of thousands of types of bacteria, researchers believe that ideal bacterial species to make synthetic beef is found in human belly button. A sample is taken from navels of people across the country to identify bacterial species that is present in human belly.
